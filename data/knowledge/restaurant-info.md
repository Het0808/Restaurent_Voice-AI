# Saffron Courtyard Restaurant Information

> Saffron Courtyard is a fictional restaurant used as sample project data.

## Address and contact

Saffron Courtyard, 18 Riverfront Avenue, Navrangpura, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380009. Phone: +91 79 4000 1234.

## Opening hours

Monday to Friday: lunch 12:00–3:00 PM and dinner 6:30–11:00 PM. Saturday and Sunday: 12:00–11:00 PM. The kitchen accepts final orders 30 minutes before closing. Holiday hours may differ and should be confirmed by phone.

## Parking

Limited complimentary parking is available behind the building. Paid street parking and a public car park are available within approximately 300 metres. Parking spaces cannot be reserved.

## Accessibility and facilities

The main entrance and dining room are step-free. An accessible restroom is available. Guests may request a high chair. A quieter corner can be requested but is subject to availability.

## Supported languages

Service is available in English, Hindi, and Gujarati.

## Dining options

The sample restaurant provides dine-in and takeaway service. It does not provide delivery directly. Indoor seating is air-conditioned; a small covered outdoor section operates when weather permits.
