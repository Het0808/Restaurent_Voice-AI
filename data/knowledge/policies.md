# Saffron Courtyard Sample Policies

> These are fictional sample policies for development and demonstration.

## Reservation policy

Reservations are accepted up to 30 days in advance and are held for the booked party size. A reservation request is confirmed only after the restaurant system issues a confirmation code.

## Cancellation policy

Guests should cancel at least four hours before the reservation. The sample restaurant does not charge a cancellation fee for standard bookings, but repeated no-shows may limit future bookings.

## Late arrival policy

Tables are held for 15 minutes after the reservation time. Guests running late should call the restaurant. After 15 minutes, seating depends on availability.

## Group booking policy

Parties larger than eight should contact the restaurant directly. Groups of 12 or more may require a deposit and a fixed menu agreed with staff.

## Outside food policy

Outside food and drink are not permitted, except a celebration cake approved in advance or food required for an infant or documented medical need.

## Child seating policy

High chairs are available in limited numbers and should be requested when booking. Children must remain supervised.

## Allergen disclaimer

The kitchen handles multiple allergens in shared preparation and cooking areas. Staff can explain documented ingredients, but cannot guarantee an allergen-free meal. Guests with severe allergies should speak directly with a manager and seek appropriate medical guidance.
