# Saffron Courtyard Frequently Asked Questions

> These answers describe a fictional sample restaurant.

## Does paneer tikka contain dairy?

Yes. Paneer is a dairy product and the sample marinade contains yogurt. Cross-contact with other allergens is also possible in the shared kitchen.

## Are vegan dishes available?

Some dishes can be prepared without dairy, including vegetable biryani without raita and masala dosa without butter or ghee. Preparation requests do not eliminate cross-contact risk; speak with staff about allergies.

## Can the restaurant guarantee an allergen-free meal?

No. The shared kitchen handles dairy, gluten, nuts, peanuts, sesame, soy, and other allergens. Guests with severe allergies should speak directly with a manager.

## How long is a table held?

A reserved table is held for 15 minutes. Call the restaurant if delayed; later seating depends on availability.

## Is parking available?

Limited complimentary rear parking is available, with paid alternatives nearby. Spaces cannot be reserved.

## Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?

The main entrance and dining room are step-free and an accessible restroom is available.

## Which languages are supported?

Staff can assist in English, Hindi, and Gujarati.

## Can I bring a celebration cake?

A celebration cake may be brought only with advance approval. Other outside food is generally not permitted, with limited infant and medical exceptions.
