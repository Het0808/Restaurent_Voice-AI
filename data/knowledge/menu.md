# Saffron Courtyard Sample Menu

> This is fictional sample data for development and demonstration. Recipes and kitchen practices may change. No item is claimed to be allergen-free.

## Starters

### Paneer Tikka — ₹360

Char-grilled cubes of paneer marinated with yogurt, ginger, garlic, Kashmiri chilli, and spices, served with mint chutney. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan: no. Contains dairy. The chutney may contain dairy. May contain nuts, gluten, soy, sesame, or other allergens due to shared kitchen handling. Spice level: medium.

## Main Course

### Dal Makhani — ₹320

Slow-cooked black lentils and kidney beans finished with butter and cream. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan: no. Contains dairy. May contain gluten or nuts due to shared kitchen handling. Spice level: mild.

### Vegetable Biryani — ₹340

Basmati rice cooked with seasonal vegetables, herbs, saffron, and whole spices, served with yogurt raita. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan without raita may be requested, but cross-contact remains possible. Contains dairy when served with raita. May contain nuts. Spice level: medium.

### Masala Dosa — ₹260

Crisp fermented rice-and-lentil crepe filled with spiced potato, served with sambar and coconut chutney. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan preparation may be requested without butter or ghee. The coconut chutney may contain nuts and shared equipment creates cross-contact risk. Spice level: medium.

## Breads

### Butter Naan — ₹95

Tandoor-baked refined wheat bread brushed with butter. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan: no. Contains gluten and dairy. May contain sesame and nuts due to shared preparation areas. Spice level: none.

## Desserts

### Gulab Jamun — ₹180

Milk-solid dumplings fried and soaked in cardamom sugar syrup. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan: no. Contains dairy and gluten. May contain nuts due to garnish and shared kitchen handling. Spice level: none.

## Drinks

### Mango Lassi — ₹190

Chilled yogurt drink blended with mango pulp and cardamom. Vegetarian: yes. Vegan: no. Contains dairy. May contain nuts due to shared equipment. Spice level: none.

## Allergen guidance

Guests with allergies must inform staff before ordering. The restaurant handles dairy, gluten, nuts, peanuts, sesame, soy, and other allergens in a shared kitchen, so cross-contact is possible. Menu evidence is informational and is not medical advice.
